Twinned Characters:
Twinned characters are characters with a single token but 2 players assigned to them. The players share a seat, with whichever one of them that is currently seated being the one that can nominate and vote. More to be added.

"Phased Out" Townsfolk:
Phased Out Townsfolk do not have a seat and do not count towards the player count total, but may nominate and vote. They cannot be nominated and they cannot die.

Token Keeper:
While a Token Keeper character is on the script, players hold on to their character tokens during the game. The Token Keeper character interacts with this somehow.
